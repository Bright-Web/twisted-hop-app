Welcome to The Twisted Hop

The Twisted Hop on Ferry Road opened in November 2012. With a history in Christchurch going back to 2004, we are a craft beer gastropub with our point of difference being cask-conditioned ale. Our passion is to provide high quality products in comfortable and unpretentious surroundings. We take pride in offering a fine selection of national/international craft beers and ciders, an outstanding wine list and delicious food.

It is our belief that a pub should be at the very heart of the community and we endeavour to make everyone as comfortable as they would feel in their own home. So, whether you are already a regular or chanced upon us for the first time, we warmly welcome you.
